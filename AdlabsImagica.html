• Adlabs Imagica is India's first and only International standard Theme Park, offering fun, action, entertainment, dining and shopping at a single location.
  • Imagica is driven by a whole world of fantastical themes to bring Indian families' experiences that they have never had before.
  • In every zone you will be immersed in a new theme that will extend through every touch-point.
  • With 26 attractions & rides and 5 themed restaurants, Imagica is a place where stories come alive.

By Road:

Panvel - Adlabs Imagica(35.3 km/32 min)

Panvel
Get on Mumbai - Pune Expy in Kon from National Highway 4
Head east...98 m
Turn right towards National Highway 4...1.4 km
Pass by Enkay Heritage (on the left)
Turn left onto National Highway 4...5.3 km
Pass by Tata Electric (on the right in 350 m)
Turn left to merge onto Mumbai - Pune Expy...750 m
Follow Mumbai - Pune Expy to Pen-Khopoli Rd. Take the exit from Mumbai - Pune Expy
Merge onto Mumbai - Pune Expy...23.7 km
Take the exit towards Pen-Khopoli Rd...500 m
Drive to Khopoli - Pali Rd
Sharp right onto Pen-Khopoli Rd...550 m
Pass by Omkar Hotel (on the right in 500 m)
Take the 1st left onto Khopoli - Pali Rd...29 m
Take the 1st right to stay on Khopoli - Pali Rd...3 km
Pass by Mayuri Hotel (on the right)
That's it !!! you are at Adlabs Imagica

By Rail:
  • You can board a train from Mumbai or Pune to Khopoli and from the station use local transport to reach the park.
  • Adlabs Imagica is just 11 km from Khopoli Railway Station.
