• Suraj Water Park is an exciting water recreation paradise guaranteeing an enthralling adventure for all those who visit the park.
  • One can also find a massive figure of 'Lord Shiva' with the river Ganga flowing from his mane.
  • Suraj Water Park is indeed a perfect destination to relax and connect with nature.
  • This water park is a five times Limca Book of Records record holder and is a winner of International as well as National Awards.
  • 'Nanha Tal' is a little slide for children and offers a great deal of excitement.
  • Other fascinating slides like 'Ding-Dong', 'Labak and Zabak' and 'Sing-a-Song' are star attractions of Suraj Water Park.
  • The 'Crazy River' and 'Lazy River' are water areas that emulate the flow of real rivers, where children and adults are pulled by the current, using floats and rubber tubes as protection against the water.
  • Open from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM every day.
