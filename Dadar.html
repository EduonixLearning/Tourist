• Dadar Chowpatty beach is a small and less crowded rocky beach with little sand around. People comes here to enjoy silence, breeze, sunset view and the giant view of Bandra Worli Sea Link bridge.
  • Dadar beach is scenic with couple of attractions frequently visited by locals, at walkable distance from shivaji park.
  • An awesome view of Bandra Worli Sea Link is available with those soothing sea shore water sounds that hits the rocks.
  • Bandra Worli Sea Link
  • Dadar Market, Near Station
  • Shivaji Park Garden (Just an Open Public Garden and Nothing more).
  • Famous Siddhivinayak Temple at Prabhadevi.
  • One can have an awesome view of Bandra Worli Sea Link.
  • In north side of beach some refreshment can be found like Bhelpuri wala, chaat corner along with Horse riding option.
  • One can visit Shivaji Park Garden (Just an Open Public Garden and Nothing more) which is located near by the beach.
  • The best time to visit the Dadar Chowpatty is between the months of September and April.
  • DO NOT try to get into beach water for swim as the waves and the sand is unstable, since years there are many accident cases of youngsters getting into beach for a swim and never coming back.
  • The tide, sand and unstable depth during high / low tides are dangerous.
  • Another this is if you are alone as couple, make it a point to move away by evening as this isolated beach might attract some trouble.
