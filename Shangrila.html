• Shangrila Water Park is a wonderful place to enjoy with friends and family.
  • Aptly located in the Mumbai-Nashik Highway, the Shangrila Water Park is blessed with a peaceful ambience.
  • The fun slides, pools, swings and rides are a great hit among the children.
  • One can enjoy getting drenched while dancing to the tunes of the DJ during the rain-dance events.
  • The 'Family Slide' is one of the major attractions of this place and so are the kiddie-slides, Jacuzzis, roller coasters, bumper cars and the huge swimming pool.
  • A special play area is dedicated to children, where toys are kept for their amusement.
  • The children can play with 'Toy Horses', 'Bike Riders', video games, swings and merry-go-rounds that are set up in the children's play area.
  • Visitors can also access the pool tables and card game facilities at the water park.
  • Open from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM on all days.
  • Water Park+Amusement Park+Food Package(Veg):

    1. Rs. 700/- for children
    2. Rs. 750/- for adults
